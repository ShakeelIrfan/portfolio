FAQ 06

Common questions.

How long does a project take?
+
Essential and Premium projects take 2–3 weeks. Luxury projects with Three.js take 4–6 weeks depending on scope and how fast feedback comes back.
Do you use WordPress or templates?
+
Never. Every site is custom-coded using React. No WordPress, no Squarespace, no page builders. Better performance, more flexibility, and a product that's actually yours.
What's included after launch?
+
Post-launch support is included on every tier — 30 to 90 days. Bug fixes, minor updates, deployment help. Ongoing maintenance available on retainer.
Do you work with US clients remotely?
+
Yes. Most of our work is with US clients — med spas, salons, studios. Email and video call. Payments online. Timezone differences have never been an issue.
How do we get started?
+
Fill the form below or DM on Instagram. We'll reply in 24 hours with a proposal. 50% deposit upfront, remainder on delivery.
